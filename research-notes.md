# Door Expert Research Notes — Key Findings

## Room-based navigation (Section 6)
Source: UX Research for Navigation.md (line 7-9)
- Room-based navigation works well as SECONDARY discovery path, not primary
- IKEA, West Elm, Daltile, Marazzi use room-led content alongside primary categories
- Rooms: Kupatilo, Dnevna soba, Hodnik, Kuhinja, Spavaca soba, Stan po mjeri
- Must show atmospheric image + relevant products from multiple categories
- Cross-sell opportunity: bathroom = tiles + washbasin + interior door

Source: Zapisnik sastanka (meeting notes)
- "Room-based navigation: user picks a room (living room, bathroom, hallway...) 
  and sees an atmospheric image + relevant products below"
- Confirmed rooms: dnevna soba, kupatilo, hodnik, kuhinja, spavaca soba

Source: Website UX Research (line 55-57)
- "Vizuelna inspiracija pokreće interesovanje" — room nav is inspiration-first
- Cross-sell logic: bathroom renovation first, then doors
- Typical purchase: ~5 interior doors per apartment, 1 glass, 1 bathroom door

## Brand strip (Section 7)
Source: Business Facts
- Ceramic brands: New Tiles, Tau Ceramica, Arcana Ceramica, Ceramica Ribesalbes
- Washbasin brand: Bathco (Spain only)
- Spanish origin is the single biggest selling advantage — must be prominent
- Brand strip placed LOWER on page — visible but not dominating top
- Door brands NOT highlighted (factories in Russia/Ukraine)

Source: Trust Signals DoorExpert.md
- "Poznati brendovi keramike i sanitarija (španski proizvođači) trebaju se 
  predstaviti preko logoa i naslova... 'direktan uvoz iz Španije — garancija 
  originalnog kvaliteta'."

## Instagram feed (Section 8)
Source: Zapisnik sastanka
- "Instagram feed pri dnu stranice (iznad footera), s automatskim ažuriranjem"
- "Instagram donosi 70% kupaca — bez njega ne bi bilo prodaje"
- NEVER at top — don't send users off-site early

## Footer (Section 9)
Source: Website UX Research (line 43)
- Must have: phone, email, address, map, working hours, brands
- "Contact us" must have at minimum phone and email, not just a form
- Physical address as trust signal for new residents

## Color system (confirmed)
